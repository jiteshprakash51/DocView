Think Grow Rich

"Think and Grow Rich" is a classic personal development book by Napoleon Hill that outlines thirteen steps to achieving success and wealth, both financial and personal. It emphasizes the importance of self-confidence, goal-setting, and unleashing one's potential. The book has been highly influential in shaping modern views on success and is considered a foundational text in the self-help genre.
